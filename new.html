Kerala is a popular destination for both domestic as well as foreign tourists.
Kerala is well known for its beaches, backwaters in Alappuzha and Kollam, mountain ranges and wildlife sanctuaries.
Other popular attractions in the state include the beaches at Kovalam, Muzhappilangad, Bekal and Kappad.
