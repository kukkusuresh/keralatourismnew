KASARGOD

Kasaragod is home to several forts which include Arikady fort, Bekal Fort,
Chandragiri Fort, and Hosdurg Fort. Bekal Fort is also the largest fort in Kerala.

Chandragiri Fort

Chandragiri Fort is a fort built in the 17th century, situated in Kasaragod .

RanipuramHilla

Ranipuram is a renowned tourist destination in the northern tip of Kerala.
Located in Kasaragod, it is situated 750 m above sea leve.
