Illikkal Mala

Illikkal Mala comprises three hills
each rising up to 3,400 ft above sea leve.
Each of the hills has a peculiar shape.

Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary

Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary (also known as Vembanad Bird Sanctuary)
is situated at Kumarakom in Kottayam taluk of Kottayam district in
the Indian state of Kerala

Malarikkal

Malarikkal is a small tourism hamlet located in Thiruvarpu panchayat in Kottayam town of Kerala

Marmala waterfall

Marmala waterfall is about 40 meter in height
plunging into a 12-meter deep pool and feeds the River Teekoy.
